How Often Should You Deep Clean Your Carpets?

Regular carpet cleaning extends the life of your flooring and improves indoor air quality. Most homes should have carpets professionally cleaned every 12-18 months, but high-traffic areas, homes with pets or children, or allergy sufferers may benefit from cleaning every 6-12 months. Professional steam cleaning removes deep-seated dirt, allergens, and bacteria that vacuuming can't reach, keeping your home healthier and your carpets looking newer longer.
